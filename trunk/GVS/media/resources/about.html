About Gadget Visual Storyboard

The Gadget Visual Storyboard is part of the FAST Project, partially funded by the European Commision by its 7th Framework Programme (FP7)

FAST aims at providing an innovative visual programming environment that will facilitate the development of next-generation composite user interfaces. It constitutes a novel approach to application composition and business process definition from a top-down user-centric perspective.

If you find any bug or interesting feature that could be added to the GVS, please write an email to the mailing list or post a new idea to our feedback forum. You can also follow us on Facebook.
